10 Steps to Success for New Agents – Start here!

1) Reach out to your State’s Division of Insurance provide them with your fingerprints, Xcel certificate, and your state exam passing score (you will receive that information from your  testing center).

2) Wait for your National Producer Number (NPN) from your state (typically takes 2-6 weeks to issue).

3) Tell your upline ASAP your National Producer Number to start your contracting process.

4) Setup HCMS and SureLC with your upline – put as much information as possible.

5) Book a contracting appointment with Shannon to finish your contracting process: Schedule now!

6) Plug into our socials to stay up-to-date with FFL: Plug-in now!

7) While waiting for contracting, complete the General Life Bootcamp and IUL and Annuity Bootcamp

8) Schedule a meeting with your upline to go over your schedule, products, lead spend, and scripts.

9) Login to Gateway and go to ‘LeadCENTER’ to buy leads – Gateway Login

10) Start Protecting Families – Join live Zoom Dials or head to your Nearest Office and start calling those leads!
